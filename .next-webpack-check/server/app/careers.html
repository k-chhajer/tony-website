Meaningful work with real responsibility

We are looking for calm, patient-focused team members who can combine strong safety standards with respectful communication and dependable transport support.

Minimum Qualifications

  • Valid BLS certification or CPR Level HCP
  • Ontario G Class Driver's License
  • Ability to lift 250+ lbs with a partner using stretcher or stair chair
  • Clean driver's abstract
  • Criminal Record Check with Vulnerable Sector Screening
  • Strong verbal and written communication in English
  • Calm under pressure

Health & Immunization Requirements

  • MMR
  • DPT
  • Polio
  • Varicella
  • Hepatitis B
  • COVID-19 (minimum two doses)
  • TB Skin Test (Step 1 within 12 months)
